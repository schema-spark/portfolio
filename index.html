VERSATILE

If there's one word that describes my work experience, it's VERSATILE. Being somewhat transient as a child taught me the to adjust to most any circumstance and environment, and find a way to succeed. I see this as a gift now, and tend to thrive in new and challenging situations.
  • I have a BA in Graphic Design and have worked in advertising and freelance.
  • I own and operate a photography business.
  • I have created sculptures for International businesses and events.
  • I have held a state teaching license in Indiana and Florida
  • I have taught design and photography classes for adults and high school students.
Feel free to review my linkedIn for a more detailed description of each.

CREATIVE

I'm always evolving; searching for beauty and a more efficient, yet enjoyable route.
    There is inspiration everywhere- but you have to be open to seeing it, and then from a new angle.
I'm always working, always improving, always reinventing, always searching, always creating.

PERSPECTIVE

Perspective is determined by your viewpoint or point-of-view. In light of this, I begin a project by looking from a variety of angles. In my workflow, I use a series of checkpoints to evaluate my chosen approach, and whether it is fitting into my design plan and coming across in my work.
